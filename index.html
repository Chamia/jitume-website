The Problem

Kenya's casual labour market
runs on WhatsApp and luck

🏢
For Employers
  • Calling 10 people on WhatsApp to fill one shift
  • No-shows with no recourse and no backup plan
  • No vetting — you don't know who's arriving
  • Cash payments with no record or audit trail
  • Good workers lost, bad ones rehired by mistake
👷
For Workers
  • Work found through referrals and WhatsApp groups
  • Paid in cash — late, short, or sometimes not at all
  • No employment record and no portable reputation
  • No contract, no WIBA coverage, no protection
  • No way to prove work history for loans or credit
